Ricky's Story

Ricky was a bright, kind, and spirited soul whose light touched everyone he met. Born with a rare condition, Ricky refused to let it define him. With boundless determination and a heart full of hope, he embraced life with courage, compassion, and an unshakeable sense of purpose.

Running became more than a passion for Ricky—it became a movement. His love for the sport and his community united people from all walks of life. Inspired by his legacy, the Run4Ricky Foundation was established to carry his mission forward.

Today, we run in Ricky’s name to champion awareness, foster inclusion, and empower those facing challenges. Through each event and every stride, we honor Ricky's memory by uplifting others and continuing the journey he so bravely began.
