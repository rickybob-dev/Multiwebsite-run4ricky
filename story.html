Ricky’s Journey

Ricky was born with cerebral palsy, but from a young age, his spirit and determination knew no bounds. Despite physical challenges, he found joy in running small laps in his backyard, inspiring his family and friends.

Milestones & Timeline

  • 2001: Born in Verona, Italy.
  • 2010: First community fun run at age 9.
  • 2015: Completed his first 5K with custom support equipment.
  • 2020: Launched Run4Ricky annual fundraiser.
  • 2024: Expanded programs to support families across Europe.
